Recipes for Kat!

Hello, my name is Kat. I have created this list of recipes to help me eat better. Here are some things about me and why I chose to create this recipe list.

  • I don't eat much but I want to eat more.
  • I don't have much of an appetite for food in general so well put together meals are best
  • I really like fruit!
  • Seasonings make everything taste so much better
  • Eating healthy is a requirement but not but something I should stress over

Here are listed some good breakfast recipes, most contain eggs though, but I seem to be fine to eat a lot of them in a day.

  1. Overnight Oats
  2. Tortilla Breakfast Wrap
  3. Egg-in-a-Hole Sandwich
  4. Avocado Breakfast Sandwich
  5. Best Breakfast Potatoes Ever

All five recipes are perfect for breakfast.

A good side dish to eat with any of these is some diced tomatoes and an avocado, salt and peppered with olive oil.
